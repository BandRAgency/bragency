Our Offer

Course of a campaign

Our work consist of creating customized campaigns and advertising strategies to represent you or your company on social media platforms.

5 - 7 days                           

Beginning

In the beginning you tell us in a personal conversation what exactly you want to promote and what goals you want to reach.
We start by collecting information about you and your company and the find a way to position you or your company properly on social media. During the developing process we keep a close contact with our clients in order to get feedback and thoughts on ideas. After the developing process we will present to you the final campaign and discuss last details. The campaign will be launched once our client gives us his affirmation.

7 - 28 days                           

Launch

After the launch we will give you constant updates about the status and the next steps in the campaign. During the campaign you will have the ability to make adjustments if wished. The satisfaction of our clients is our first priority.

1 - 2 days                         

Résumé

At the end we will discuss with you the results and help you with the next smart steps for you and your brand.

An average campaign takes approximatly 2-5 weeks 

All numbers are costumizeable and can be changes to suit your demand
